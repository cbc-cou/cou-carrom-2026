Official Rules & Regulations

Official framework published by the COU Sports Organizing Committee (v1.2).

1. Team Composition

  • Each team must strictly consist of 3 staff members: **2 Male and 1 Female player**.
  • Roster substitutions are strictly prohibited unless approved in writing due to a medical emergency.

2. Mandatory Tie Sequence

Every league match requires all 3 segments to be fully played:

  1. Game 01: Men's Doubles (Male A + Male B)
  2. Game 02: Mixed Match (Female + Male A or B)
  3. Game 03: Men's Singles (Remaining Male Player)

3. Circular Tie-Breaker Protocol

If a 3-way group tie occurs (all teams finish 1-1), seeding is decided via:

  1. **Total Games Won** (Out of 6 total games played across group matches).
  2. **Net Points System** (Cumulative board margins tracked continuously).

4. Illegal Queen Penalty

  • Pocketing the **final piece** of the opposition before the Queen is covered yields an **immediate game forfeit**.
  • Victory points for all remaining pieces on board are automatically awarded to the opposition.
  • An additional **+5 point fixed bonus** is assigned to the opposition (and the violating team gets the matching negative value).
